# IPL Fantasy League 2026 — Rules Reference

> Source: BCCI IPL Fantasy League Terms & Conditions (iplt20.com)
> Last updated: 2026-03-23

## Squad Selection (Season Long Fantasy)

- **11 players** from the pool of real-world players
- **100 credits** budget
- **Role limits:**
  - Batters (BAT): 3 min, 6 max
  - All-rounders (AR): 1 min, 4 max
  - Wicket-keepers (WK): 1 min, 4 max
  - Bowlers (BOWL): 3 min, 6 max
- **Overseas limit:** max 4 overseas players
- **Franchisee spread:** max 7 players from a single IPL team
- **Captain:** 2x points per matchday
- **Vice-Captain:** 1.5x points per matchday
- C/VC can be changed unlimited times (takes effect next matchday)

## Transfer Rules

| Phase | Transfers allowed |
|-------|-------------------|
| Before joining | Unlimited changes |
| Match 1 to Match 70 (league stage) | 160 transfers total |
| After Match 70 to Qualifier 1 | Unlimited transfers |
| Qualifier 1 to Final | 10 transfers |

- Transfers made during a match take effect for the **next** match only
- Can edit team until the official start time of the match

## Boosters (6 types)

| Booster | Effect | Uses |
|---------|--------|------|
| **Triple Captain** | Captain earns 3x points instead of 2x | 2 |
| **Wildcard** | Unlimited transfers within 100-credit budget | 1 |
| **Double Points** | Entire team earns 2x points for a match | 2 |
| **Free Hit** | Unlimited transfers, no budget cap; team reverts after match | 1 |
| **Foreign Stars** | All overseas players earn 2x points for a match | 2 |
| **Indian Warrior** | All Indian players earn 2x points for a match | 2 |

## Playoffs League

- Boosters **disabled** in Playoffs Leagues (except Free Hit and Wildcard)
- Boosters remain available in Global, Favourite Team, and Private Leagues

## Tiebreaker

1. Captain points (higher wins)
2. Vice-Captain points (higher wins)
3. Earlier submission time

## Match Abandonment

- If abandoned before start: system treats as ended; transfers/boosters not refunded
- If abandoned after start: points earned until abandonment are retained; transfers/boosters not refunded

## User Moderated Leagues

- Max 20 private leagues per user
- Admin can remove users before they accumulate points
- If admin leaves, next user who joined becomes admin
